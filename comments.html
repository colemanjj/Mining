Comments on Wisconsin Mining Related Legislation
  • GLIFWC written testimony on SB24/AB24. SB24/AB24 changes regulations for permitting activities that impact lakes and rivers, particularly permitting under Chapter 30 of the Wisconsin statutes. Submitted October 28, 2011.
  • GLIFWC written testimony on AB426. AB426 creates seperate regulations for permitting of iron mines. Submitted December 14, 2011.
  • GLIFWC written testimony on AB463/SB386.  AB463/SB386 modifies state wetland regulations. Submitted January 23, 2012.
  • GLIFWC written testimony on AB1/SB1.  AB1/SB1 creates seperate regulations for permitting of iron mines. Submitted January 23, 2013.
